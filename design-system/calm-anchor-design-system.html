Calm Anchor Color System

A comprehensive, trauma-informed color system for a PTSD recovery and nervous system regulation app. Built on earthy green and warm brown brand anchors. Expanded to cover nervous system states, survival responses, exercise types, mood tracking, crisis UI — and the Window of Tolerance — all drawn directly from the Bella Wolf CPTSD Toolkit.

4 — Survival Response Colors ✦ New

The toolkit's four survival responses — Fight, Flight, Freeze, Fawn — each get a distinct, carefully differentiated color. Used in the nervous system map, the self-soothing menu builder, and the daily check-in to help users identify their state without clinical language.

⚡ Fight
Anger · Raised voice · Jaw tension · Defensiveness · Need for control

--sr-flight / --sr-flight-bg

🧊 Freeze
Numbness · Dissociation · Shutdown · Paralysis · Inability to speak
--sr-freeze / --sr-freeze-bg

What is the Window of Tolerance? ▾
The Window of Tolerance describes the zone of arousal in which a person can function most effectively. When you are within your window, you feel calm, grounded, and able to think clearly.
How do breathing exercises help? ▾
Breathing exercises activate the parasympathetic nervous system, which helps calm the body and mind. The 4-7-8 technique is especially effective for reducing anxiety.
When should I seek help? ▾
If you are experiencing thoughts of self-harm, feeling unable to cope, or if your symptoms are significantly impacting your daily life, please reach out to a mental health professional or use the crisis resources in this app.
